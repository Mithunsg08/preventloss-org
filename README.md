## About

PreventLoss.org is an independent educational platform dedicated to helping businesses improve operational performance through practical, easy-to-understand resources.

The website publishes in-depth articles, guides, templates, calculators, and best practices covering:

- Loss Prevention
- Inventory Management
- Procurement
- Internal Audit
- Cost Control
- Operations Excellence

Our mission is simple:

> Help businesses protect what they have worked hard to build.

The platform is completely free and designed for:

- Business Owners
- Operations Managers
- Retail Managers
- Warehouse Managers
- Procurement Teams
- Internal Auditors
- Finance Professionals
- Loss Prevention Professionals
- Students
- Anyone interested in improving business operations

More about the project: https://preventloss.org/about.html :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
